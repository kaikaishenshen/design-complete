## Design Context

**Design Complete** is a unified frontend design quality system that integrates taste-skill (aesthetic taste) and impeccable (structural rigor).

When working on this project, apply the design rules from:
- `src/skills/design-taste/SKILL.md` — aesthetic and visual quality
- `src/skills/impeccable/SKILL.md` — structural and engineering quality
- `src/skills/design-complete/SKILL.md` — orchestrator pipeline

### Key Design Principles
- 4-phase pipeline: taste → build → check → refine
- Conflict resolution: P0(Engineering) > P1(Aesthetic) > P2(UX) > P3(Style)
- All documentation must be available in 4 languages (en, zh-CN, ja, ko)
- Third-party attribution required for taste-skill (MIT) and impeccable (Apache 2.0)
